Soyan

Soyan is a privacy-focused cryptocurrency designed to offer enhanced security and inclusivity for all users. It utilizes a Proof of Share system to ensure that everyone can participate in the network without needing expensive hardware.

How It Works

Soyan employs innovative privacy hashing techniques that anonymize transactions, ensuring that user identities remain protected while maintaining transparency on the blockchain.

Why Soyan?

In a world where privacy is increasingly at risk, Soyan provides a decentralized solution that prioritizes user privacy, making it essential for anyone looking to transact securely online.
